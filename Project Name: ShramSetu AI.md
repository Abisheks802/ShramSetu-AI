Project Name: ShramSetu AI

Problem Statement:
A large portion of India's workforce, especially blue-collar, gig, and contractual workers, lack awareness about their labor rights, ESIC benefits, Provident Fund eligibility, gratuity rules, and complaint mechanisms. Language barriers and low digital literacy prevent access to official information.

Objective:
To develop a multilingual AI-powered conversational assistant that educates Indian workers about their legal rights, compliance benefits, and eligibility criteria in simple, regional languages through voice and text interfaces.

Target Users:
- Gig workers (delivery, ride-share)
- Factory workers
- Contractual employees
- Domestic workers
- Small business employees

1. Multilingual support (Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, Marathi â€“ scalable to 22 languages)
2. Voice-to-text interface for low literacy users
3. ESIC eligibility checker
4. PF contribution calculator
5. Gratuity eligibility guidance
6. Complaint draft generator
7. Employer compliance checklist

AI Components:
- NLP-based intent detection
- Large Language Model for simplified explanation
- Rule-based compliance engine
- Speech-to-text and text-to-speech integration

Architecture:
- Frontend: Web app + WhatsApp chatbot
- Backend: Python-based API server
- AI Layer: LLM + compliance logic module
- Database: Worker query logs + labor law dataset
- Cloud Hosting: AWS EC2/Lambda

Impact:
- Increases labor law awareness
- Reduces exploitation
- Improves compliance transparency
- Aligns with Digital India and Viksit Bharat vision

Future Scope:
- Government portal integration
- AI-based compliance risk scoring
- Employer-side dashboard
